How Maryland calculates your limit: Both dried flower and THC products draw from one shared percentage pool. % used = (flower bought ÷ flower certified) + (THC bought ÷ THC certified). Remaining = certified amount × (1 − % used). Purchases expire from the rolling window 30 days after purchase date, freeing up that percentage.

Data saves automatically to your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to any server — all calculations happen in your browser. Export JSON regularly as a backup. Source code available on GitHub.
